3Reels — Your #1 Free Reach Tool

In 2026, Reels get far more free reach than photos. Aim for 3–4 reels/week, 7–15 seconds, with trending audio. Film on your phone — no editing skills needed (use the free CapCut app).

10 ready reel ideas

  • 🛍️ “Plain cover → YOUR branded cover” — same price reveal (your hero message).
  • 🖨️ Satisfying printing process — paper running through, stacking covers.
  • 📦 Bulk order packed & ready — “500 covers for [shop type], delivered today”.
  • 🏪 “Shops in Udumalpet using KRAFT·Vibe” — quick montage of branded covers at counters.
  • 🎁 Wedding return-gift transformation — plain jute bag → printed with family name + date.
  • 🌿 “Plastic vs Paper” — drop plastic, show kraft cover, eco message.
  • 📏 Size guide — 1kg / 2kg / 5kg covers lined up.
  • ✍️ “Send us your shop name → get a sample” — show a sample being made.
  • 💍 Function combo packing — gift + bag + haldi-kumkum assembled.
  • ⭐ Before a festival — “Order your Deepavali / wedding covers now”.
Reel formula: Hook in first 2 seconds (“Same price. Your name on it.”) → show the product → end with a clear CTA (“WhatsApp 7904505226 for a free sample”). Always add a location tag: Udumalpet and 1 line of on-screen text.

6Free Local Growth Tactics

  • WhatsApp first. Post a daily Status (covers, offers). Make a broadcast list of shop owners and send a monthly update. Set up your WhatsApp Business catalogue with products + prices.
  • DM local shops directly: 10 shops/day with a short message + sample offer (see template below). Your Business Manager already has ~70 Udumalpet leads ready.
  • Join & post in local Facebook groups: “Udumalpet Buy & Sell”, “Tiruppur Business”, “Coimbatore Wedding Planners”. Share return-gift posts (where allowed).
  • Facebook Marketplace — list “Custom Return Gift Bags” and “Branded Paper Covers” (free listings, local buyers find you).
  • Collaborate (free): tag & partner with wedding planners, photographers, mandapams, cake shops. They send you return-gift / packaging orders; you tag them back.
  • Ask for tags: every happy customer — request a Story tag or a Google review. Repost it (free social proof).
  • Festivals & wedding season — post 2 weeks early (Deepavali, Pongal, wedding muhurtham dates). That’s when orders spike.

9Free Tools & What To Track

Free tools

  • Canva — posters, carousels, price lists (free templates).
  • CapCut — reel editing, trending templates, captions.
  • Meta Business Suite — schedule + post to FB & IG together, see insights.
  • Google Business Profile — local search + Maps.
  • CapCut/Canva backgrounds — use your photos; keep brand colours (green, kraft, red).

Check weekly (in Insights)

  • Reach (how many saw you) — should climb week over week.
  • Best reel — repeat that style.
  • Profile visits → WhatsApp clicks — the real business number.
  • Followers matter less than messages/orders. Focus on orders.
Golden rule: consistency beats perfection. 4 simple phone reels every week for 3 months will outperform one “perfect” post a month. Just keep posting + DM local shops daily.
